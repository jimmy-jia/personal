FAQ

Here are some often asked questions for web developers.

  • How long does it take to create a website

    • This depends on a lot of things, such as the content, requested design, features, and many other factors that can drastically affect the timeframe of a project. For simple sites, a couple days is usually enough for a finished product. The larger a project is, the harder it is to say about a time frame.

  • How much does it cost to keep my website up?

    • To actually have your website online you'll need two basic things. First off is a website domain. These are how people access your website. You can purchase them through various online stores, with a year cost that depends on the domain suffix (.com, .net, .us, etc). Second is the website hosting. Hosting is what keeps your website's files for people to access. Hosting can be purchased from a lot of providers, which have plans that range from a month to a few years per payment.
